Print projects have a real gravity: you will hold in your hands physical representation of all your hard work, and with it, the satisfaction of permanence. However, this means you need to be extra careful in your editing process. Any mistakes will be forever, too!

Digital projects are, in many ways, less restricted than the physical projects, because there is less difference in cost of materials. Instead, the question becomes one of access.
